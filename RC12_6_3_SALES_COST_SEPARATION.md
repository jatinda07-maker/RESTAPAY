# RC12.6.3 Sales and Cost Separation

- Toast Total Sales is sourced only from Toast sales rows.
- Aggregate Product Mix rows such as Total, Net Sales, Gross Sales, and Subtotal are excluded from Food and Alcohol department totals.
- Food Sales and Alcohol Sales are revenue only.
- Direct Food Cost and Direct Alcohol Cost are purchases only.
- Payroll and shared overhead remain separate operating costs.
- Operating Profit remains: Toast Total Sales - operating payroll - vendor/invoice/business spending.
