# RC8.1 Clickable Summary Cards

All major summary/KPI cards now open the records behind their totals.

Implemented on:
- Dashboard (existing drill-down retained)
- Payroll: Operating Payroll, Server Tips, Cash Paid, Check Paid, Tips Withheld
- Sales: Net Sales, Cash, Credit, Tips
- Expenses: Total, Cash, Check/Credit, ACH
- Invoices: Net Spend, Top Category, Open/Unpaid, Rebates/Credits
- Menu Costing: Menu Items, Estimated Sales, Food Cost, At-Risk Items
- Reports: cards switch directly to the corresponding detailed report
- Price Increase: cards open/filter the related detail list

Cards preserve the existing color palette and icon style. Production build completed successfully.
