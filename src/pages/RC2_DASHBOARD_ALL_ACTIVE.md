# RESTAPAY RC2 Dashboard Activation

This update makes the dashboard controls and summaries interactive.

## Activated
- Cash Flow, Prime Cost, Labor Mix, and Food Cost command tiles
- All KPI cards
- Food Cost, Alcohol Sales, Labor, Prime Cost, Net Profit, Average Check, and Reconciliation strip tiles
- All major section headers
- Cash Position rows
- Profit & Loss rows
- Sales Performance rows
- Food and Alcohol department rows
- Vendor and business expense recent rows
- Category pills and cost-page navigation

Every active item either opens a reconciled detail modal or navigates to the related full page.

- Production Vite build completed successfully.
