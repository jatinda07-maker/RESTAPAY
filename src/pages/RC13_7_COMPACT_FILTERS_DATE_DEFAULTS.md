# RC13.7 Compact Filters, Fast Entry, and Date Defaults

- Every date-filtered workspace opens month-to-date (first day of current month through today).
- Sales and Payroll include visible dropdown filters adjacent to the search/date controls.
- Search controls use a contrasting pale-gold treatment so they are easy to locate.
- Text and numeric inputs select their contents on click or Tab; zero defaults clear for immediate entry.
- Sales, Payroll, Expenses, and Invoices are displayed in ascending date order.
- Payroll history is redesigned as compact, scannable rows with reduced repeated spacing and labels.
