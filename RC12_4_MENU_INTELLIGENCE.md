# RC12.4 Menu Intelligence

Adds a dedicated Menu Intelligence sidebar page with:

- Most sold items
- Least sold items and their recipe cost/profit
- Highest-cost plates
- Highest and lowest total gross profit items
- Most expensive ingredients by sold-item cost impact
- Menu engineering matrix: Stars, Plowhorses, Puzzles, Dogs
- Date presets, custom dates, search, and category filtering
- Clickable item popups with sales, recipe cost, profit, margin, ingredient list, vendor, and recipe subtotal

Calculations use Toast Product Mix menu items and the saved recipe lines in Menu Costing. Accurate plate profit requires approved recipe quantities and current ingredient costs.
